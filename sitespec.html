Pizza Ordering Website Specifications

Overview

The Pizza Ordering Website allows users to order pizzas online with a user-friendly interface. Customers can customize their pizzas, choose from various sizes, crust types, and toppings, and place their orders for delivery or pickup.

Inspiration

My goal in this project was to create a combination of a traditional pizza restaurant in my country Luca Traditie and Domino's pizza only because of how easy it is to order from them.

Details about my website

  • Client or subject: Pizza
  • Title: Pizza Paradise
  • Purpose: To make a nice website where the user can see the big range of products and also an easy ordering system for the customer
  • Target audience: Any person that is hungry for a pizza, any age mostly.
  • Content of the website: Pizza and the story behind the specific traditional pizza maker.

Description

The business: A traditional pizza restaurant that is owned by a family from generations.

What is the topic? The topic is to create a company that would serve well clients, that would listen very carefully to their needs, this is why I created the feedback page. A page that I wish would exist on any possible company, because there are always so many things to improve.

Who is the target audience: Any person, any sex, any age group. Basically everyone that is hungry for some good pizza.

Existing website: Yes! My goal was to combine two pizza companies, one from my home country, Luca Luca Traditie and the other one being Domino's pizza. The idea behind this was to bring to the customer both the traditional feeling, but also the easiness to order a pizza and to have it delivered quite shortly.

Features

  • Menu: Display a list of pizzas with images, descriptions, ingredients and prices.
  • Offers: Highlight any ongoing promotions or deals.
  • Ordering System: Users can add pizzas and the system will tell them how much it is and eventually it will give an email/message to the kitchen of the pizza restaurant to prepare as fast as possible.
  • The story of the pizza shop: The user can see here how the business started and how it continued to get larger with time.
  • Feedback: An essential page for every commercial entity. Why? Because it gives valuable feedback that can help improve the company.
  • Contact: A very simple form, where the user or any other potential partner can contact the owner.

Conclusion

This specification outlines the requirements and features necessary for developing a pizza ordering website. The goal is to create a seamless and enjoyable ordering experience for users.
